CLASSIX clustered 900 data points with 2 features.
The radius parameter was set to 0.25 and minPts was set to 10.
As the provided data was auto-scaled by a factor of 1/1.42,
points within a radius R=0.25*1.42=0.36 were grouped together.
In total, 1426 distances were computed (1.6 per data point).
This resulted in 15 groups, each with a unique group center.
These 15 groups were subsequently merged into 4 clusters. 

For a visualisation of the clusters, use .explain(plot=True).
To explain the clustering of individual data points, use
.explain(index1) or .explain(index1,index2) with data indices.
